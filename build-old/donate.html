    Charleston Area Justice Ministry (CAJM)

    Congregational Financial Commitment Form

    Purpose: CAJM shall build a Congregation-Based Justice Ministry, including fundraising and member recruitment.

    Mission: CAJM shall build a powerful Congregation-Based Justice Ministry capable of negotiating the interests of our communities and committed to doing justice. The organization shall be comprised of a diverse group of religious congregations. Having been founded by religious congregations, the organization shall be based on the values of justice and compassion as shared by our faith traditions.

    Financial Commitment: To build a strong and sustainable financial foundation for the organization, CAJM has developed a goal for each congregation to invest 1% of their operating budget each year in this Congregation-Based Justice Ministry.
